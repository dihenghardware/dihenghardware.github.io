About Our Company

Diheng Hardware is located in Chang'an Town, Dongguan — China’s famous mold industry base. We specialize in R&D, production and sales of precision pin gauges, mold components, precision shafts, turning bushings, non-standard fasteners, electronic hardware and pneumatic parts.

We introduce high-precision grinding and turning equipment from Germany and Japan. Custom non-standard parts are available with high tolerance up to ±0.001mm, high hardness and wear resistance.

Our products are widely used in mold manufacturing, 3C electronics, home appliances, auto parts, optoelectronics and automation equipment. Standard items in stock, fast delivery for custom orders. Welcome global business cooperation.
