About Us

Diheng Hardware is a professional pin gauge expert located in Changan Town, Dongguan, the famous mold industry base in China.

We introduce high-precision grinding equipment from Germany and Japan. Our pin gauge tolerance reaches ±0.001mm, featuring high hardness, wear resistance and stable shape. We supply standard stock items and accept OEM custom drawings & samples.

Our products are widely exported to North America, Europe, Japan and other regions, applied in mold making, 3C electronics, auto parts and automation equipment. Sincere cooperation with global buyers is warmly welcomed.
