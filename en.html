About Us

Diheng Hardware is a professional manufacturer of precision pin gauges and mold parts, located in Chang'an Town, Dongguan, China.

We produce carbide pin gauges, HSS pin gauges, ceramic pin gauges and custom non-standard pin gauges, widely used in mold, electronics, plastic and hardware industries. High precision, stable quality and on-time delivery are our advantages.
