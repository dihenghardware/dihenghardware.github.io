About Us

Dongguan Changan Diheng Hardware Mold Accessories Processing Factory is a professional manufacturer of precision pin gauges and mold parts, located in Chang'an Town, Dongguan, China.

We specialize in producing carbide pin gauges, high-speed steel pin gauges, ceramic pin gauges, and custom non-standard pin gauges, widely used in mold, electronics, plastic, and hardware precision inspection industries. We are committed to high precision, stable quality, and on-time delivery, accepting drawings or samples for customization.
